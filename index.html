About Us

Discover the origin of our passion for the sea and how we managed to harness the most untamable energy on the planet.

2024 Year of Foundation

Born from the relentless heartbeat of the ocean

Ener-Wave was founded by a prominent group of naval engineers and climate scientists in 2024. The company emerged from a simple yet powerful revelation off the Patagonian coast: while solar energy relied on daylight and wind energy depended on unstable gusts, the ocean waves never stopped. Their constant ebb and flow concealed the planet's greatest untapped energy potential.

Driven by this discovery and motivated by the global climate urgency, we design advanced marine engineering solutions. We develop closed-loop wave energy converters capable of withstanding the ocean's most hostile storms, converting tidal impact into constant, emission-free electricity directly to the onshore grid.

Our Technology

We develop cutting-edge mechanical and software solutions to harness the power of the seas.

Wave Energy

Systems that harness the vertical and horizontal kinetic thrust of surface waves using heavy-duty hydropneumatic oscillating buoys.

  • High Operational Consistency (94%)
  • Zero Acoustic Emissions
  • Biodegradable Anchors

Tidal Energy

Submerged bidirectional turbine generators that harness rising and falling tidal flows in a predictable and safe manner.

  • 100% Energy Predictability
  • Marine fauna-safe design
  • Direct Underwater Transmission

Hybrid Offshore Farms

Integrated modular platforms combining floating offshore wind turbines and bifacial solar panels on the converter's surface.

  • Triple generation at a single point
  • Isolated floating substation
  • Continuous AI Satellite Monitoring
